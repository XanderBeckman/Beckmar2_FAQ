Shiny Pokemon

A shiny pokemon is a pokemon that uses a different color pallete. They are normally quite rare and highly sought after by collectors.

The odds were 1/8192. However with the release of the 6th set of mainline games the odds were increased to 1/4096. They can also be increased by using specific "hunting methods" such as the Masuda Method or using items like the Shiny Charm.

The Masuda method was named after the director for Pokemon Game devlopment: Junichi Masuda. It involves using Pokemon from two different regions to breed. The offspring will be 3 times as likely to be shiny.

The easiest way to start shiny hunting is to just dive right in. Pick a Pokemon you like and just begin encountering it, either in the wild or using the daycare.
